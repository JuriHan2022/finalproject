PROJECTÂ OVERVIEW

I started this project with the curiosity and desire to make abstract flower images. Flowers have always fascinated me because of their quality to bring joy and their timeless beauty. In some ways, flowers are an unnecessary produce for survival. Nevertheless, it has been studied and developed for centuries of human civilization and reamains as an essential element to human cultures. Flowers are used both to show love and grief -- And act as both symbols of youth and maturity. I am most drawn to flowers due to their ability to retain beauty throughout their whole life cycle and hold different meanings-- As they start to bud, when they are in full bloom, when they become dry flowers -- all aspects of the life cycle can be used as elements of symbolization and gifts. For this reason, flowers to me is one of the purest form of beauty and I hope you enjoy the collection while considering how flowers have brought moments of meaning to your life.
